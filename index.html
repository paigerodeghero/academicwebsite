About Me

Hi! My name is Paige Rodeghero and I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Clemson University. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Notre Dame under the direction of Collin McMillan. My main research interests include software engineering and source code comprehension.
I direct the Human Factors Software Engineering (HFSE) research team at Clemson. and I am currently looking for smart, self-motivated students. Please email me if you are interested in pursing a PhD.
