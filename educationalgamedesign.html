Pirate Adventure

Our game takes players on a pirate adventure where they must solve math problems to find and sink enemy ships on a battleship-style game board. Players can also earn coins to unlock powerups to enhance their skills and beat all the levels in the game.

Town Squared

You are a superhero fighting math monsters which have taken over town! Chase the monsters away with your superpowers and reclaim your home!

Mathoki Island Adventures

Mathoki Island Adventures is a game that allows player to practice their addition/subtraction, multiplication, and fraction skills while they help the Mathoki creatures settle into their new island home. Along the way, players earn gems that they can use to buy items and customize their Mathoki character.
